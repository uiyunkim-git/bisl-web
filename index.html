Research
MDAN (Multi-target Drug analysis with AI and Network biology)

Understanding the mechanisms and lead targets of natural products known to treat diseases is our primary focus. Natural products typically consist of multiple components and target various elements. The MDAN team analyzes lead compounds and targets using network-based and machine learning-based methods. Network-based analyses are optimal for comprehending MDAN and understanding the mechanisms of natural products for treatment. The MDAN team constructs networks and analyzes them to identify lead compounds and targets.

Research
MODA (MOdel-guided Data-driven Analtsis)

We are dedicated to transforming basic materials into high-value pharmaceuticals, a significant challenge in both academia and industry. Our research strategies are designed to drive innovations in biotechnology.

Research
OMICS

Multi-omics approach for the investigation of diverse diseases aims for the collective analysis of molecular-level phenotypes that can be found in multiple tissues over the whole body. We inspect the target disease in many aspects, which leads us to discover a step-by-step pathophysiology of the disease through the omics technology.
